Welcome to Escape the Underworld

This game is a university project about a game inspired by Zork. Also, I must say it is inspired in Greek Mythology.

Background

The history begins while your empire is in war with another one, unfortunately the city you live in is a strategic point, and you are a renowned soldier. Your empire is losing the war but your side had a witch, who had a vision on how to win the war: She saw a powerful object that some man could use and use it to won the war, the only problem is the object is trapped somewhere in the underworld, so you and other renowned soldiers are chosen to go find it.
